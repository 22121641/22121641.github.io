The Google Pixel 10 is a flagship phone with a 6.3 inch 120HZ oled display, Powered by the Tensor G5 processor and running on Android 16. Key features include a versatile triple-lens camera system with a 5x telephoto lens, AI-powered features like Gemini Nano, A 24+ hour battery, And 7 years of Os and security updates. It also offers a durable design with Gorilla Glass Victus 2, IP68 water resistance and advanced connectivity options. Prices start from £599.00 to purchase.

Google Pixel 9

The Google Pixel 9 features a 6.3-inch 120Hz OLED display, The new Tensor G4 processor, 12GB of Ram, and 4,700mah battery. Key features include a dual rear camera system with 50MP main and 48MP ultrawide lens, Satelite connectivity for emergencies, And the new add me feature for group photos. Security includes a Titan M2 security chip and it offers google VPN at no extra cost. Prices start from £599.00 to purchase.

Google Pixel 8Pro

The Google Pixel 8Pro is a smartphone featuring the Google Tensor G3 chip, A 6.7-inch 120Hz display, And pro-level triple rear camera system with a dedicated 5x telephoto lens.
