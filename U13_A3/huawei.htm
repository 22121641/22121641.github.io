Huawei Mate6

The Huawei Mate 6 is a flagship foldable smartphone with a focus on a slim, Lightweight design and durable build. Key features include a 7.93-inch internal display and a 6.45-inch cover screen, Both with 120HZ adaptive refresh rates, And an IPX8 water-resistant rating. Huawei Mate XT

The Huawei Mate XT is a trifold smartphone with a 6.4-inch outer display that unfolds to a 7.9-inch inner screen, and then unfolds a second time to a 10.2-inch tablet-like display. Key features include a 50MP ultra-aperture main camera, A 12MP periscope telephoto lens with 5.5x optical zoom, And a 12MP ultrawide camera. It runs on EMUI 14.2 and is powered by a 5,600 mah battery with storage options up to 1TB and 16GB RAM. Purchase prices start from £2,884 to purchase.
