Accesories

Air Pods

Apple offers several models of AirPods including the AirPods 4th generation, AirPods Pro 3rd generation and AirPods Max, Each with a distinct set of features tailored to different users needs. Key features across the product line emphasize seamless integration within the Apple ecosystem, High quality audio and intelligent functions. Prices range from AirPods pro 3 £219, AirPods4 from £119, AirPods Max £499.

Pixel Buds

Google offers two current models of Pixel Buds, The Pixel Buds Pro 2 and the Pixel Buds 2a. Both provide Android integration and hands-free Google Assistant access, But have different features such as noise cancellation and charging. Prices range from Google Pixel Buds Pro2 £174.98, Google Pixel Buds A series £89.99, Google Pixel Buds Pro £114.99.

Huawei Watch

Huawei watches are known for their advanced features like health monitoring, Extensive sport tracking with GPS and long battery life. They also offer Smartphone connectivity for notifications and Bluetooth calling and are built with durable materials like aerospace grade titanium and saphire glass on premium models. Prices range from Huawei Watch Ultimate 2 £799.99, Huawei Watch5 £299.99, Huawei Watch4 Pro space edition £549.99.

Galaxy Watch

Samsung Galaxy watches are Smartwatches with features including Health and Fitness tracking, Smartphone integration for calls and messages and a variety of apps and watch faces. Key health features include heart rate monitor, ECG and blood pressure monitoring (after calibration). Fitness tracking includes auto-detection of workouts and a running coach, While smartphone connectivity allows you to make calls and send messages directly from your wrist. Prices range from Galaxy Watch8 Classic £449, Galaxy watch Ultra £599, Galaxy Fit3 Bluetooth £44.
