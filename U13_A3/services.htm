services

Samsung.

For services and repairs for Samsung mobile phones you can get access through the official Samsung channels, Including walk-in, Mail-in and doorstep repair services as well as through authorized service partners. You can also perform some repairs yourself with the Samsung self repair program. For accidental damage, Battery replacement or theft Samsung offers its Samsung care+ insurance plan which provides certified care and uses genuine parts.

Apple.

For service and repairs on Apple mobile phones you can access this through the Apple stores, Apple authorised service providers, and by sending the device to Apple for repair. You can get support through Apples website, the Apple support app or by phone. Repair options include battery replacement, screen repair and other accidental damage coverage with costs depending on the issue and warranty status.

Google Pixel.

For services and repair for Goole pixel mobile phones you can access through official mail-in services, authorized walk-in partners and self repair options. You can start the repair process on the Google store website to determine your option for mail-in or walk-in repairs which vary based on your location and warranty status. For DIY repairs ifixit offers genuine parts and guides on repairs.

Huawei.

For services and repairs for Huawei mobile phones you can get access through Huaweis official service for cretified repairs and a wide range of third party repair shops for services like screen replacement, battery replacement and charging port repair. Official services may use genuine parts and have certified technicians while third party shops often offer fast and affordable options. To find a repair service quick check the official Huawei website.
